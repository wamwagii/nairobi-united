About Nairobi United

Proudly representing Kenya in the top football league since 1960

Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1960, Nairobi United has been a cornerstone of Kenyan football for over six decades. From our humble beginnings in the Nairobi leagues to our current status as Kenyan Premier League contenders, our journey has been defined by passion, resilience, and excellence.

We've won numerous domestic titles and proudly represented Kenya in continental competitions, establishing ourselves as one of East Africa's most respected football institutions.

Our club is built on the principles of community, excellence, and passion for the beautiful game. We take pride in developing local talent and providing exciting football for our dedicated fans.

World-Class Training Facilities

Our state-of-the-art training complex in Karen features three professional-grade pitches, modern gym facilities, sports science labs, and recovery centers.

The academy nurtures young talent through our renowned youth development program, producing some of Kenya's finest footballers through comprehensive technical and tactical training.

The 12th Man: Our Passionate Fans

Known as "The Green Army," our supporters are the heartbeat of Nairobi United. Week in and week out, they create an electric atmosphere that inspires our players and makes our stadium a fortress.

With over 50 official supporters' clubs across Kenya and East Africa, our fanbase continues to grow, united by their passion for the beautiful game and their unwavering support for the club.
